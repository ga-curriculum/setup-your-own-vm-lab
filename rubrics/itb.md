## Lab requirements

The following requirements describe the content that should be present in the submitted <code class="filepath">create-a-vm.md</code> file. Points awarded out of 100 for each piece of functionality.

### Part 1 - Setup a VM - Ubuntu

| Criteria                                                                                                                                                                  | Points |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ |
| States the OS running on the VM.                                                                                                                                          | 10     |
| Describes challenges faced while creating the VM and how they were overcome. If applicable, details of the troubleshooting steps and research efforts should be included. | 10     |
| Describes the amount of RAM and hard disk space allocated to the VM and why these amounts were chosen with accurate technical details.                                    | 10     |
| Provides a technically accurate description of what might happen if too much RAM was allocated to the VM.                                                                 | 10     |
| List the settings that were changed and the technical reasons why they were changed.                                                                                      | 10     |
| Provides an explanation for how the VM performed before and after the configuration changes.                                                                              | 10     |
| Describe a few settings that could be changed to further optimize the VM.                                                                                                 | 10     |
| Responses are organized in a clear and logical manner.                                                                                                                    | 10     |
| Markdown is valid and displays correctly in GitHub.                                                                                                                       | 10     |
| Professional tone and language is used throughout the document.                                                                                                           | 10     |
| **Total possible points**                                                                                                                                                 | 100    |
